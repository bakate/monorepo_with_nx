## Strings

```ts
const name = 'Bakate';
const age = 35;
const message = `Hello, my name is ${name} and I am ${age} years old.`;
console.log(message)  // Logs: Hello, my name is Bakate and I am 35 years old.
```

Strings are a sequence of characters that are enclosed in double quotes.
